EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is made between DataFlow Corporation ("Company") and Jessica Smith ("Employee") effective March 1, 2024.

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee is hired as Senior Software Engineer and shall perform duties including software development, code review, and technical leadership.

2. COMPENSATION
Employee shall receive an annual salary of $120,000, paid bi-weekly. Employee is eligible for annual performance bonuses at Company's discretion.

3. BENEFITS
Employee is entitled to health insurance, dental coverage, vision insurance, and 401(k) plan with 4% company matching.

4. WORK SCHEDULE
Employee agrees to work standard business hours and additional hours as required by Company needs. Overtime compensation not provided for exempt employees.

5. TERMINATION
Company may terminate Employee at any time for any reason or no reason. Employee may resign with two weeks' notice.

6. NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT
Employee agrees to maintain absolute confidentiality regarding all Company proprietary information, trade secrets, and business strategies.

7. NON-COMPETE CLAUSE
Employee agrees not to work for any competing company or start a competing business for 24 months after termination in any geographic location.

8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All inventions, ideas, and work products created by Employee during employment belong exclusively to Company, regardless of when or where created.

9. UNLIMITED LIABILITY
Employee agrees to indemnify Company against all losses, damages, and legal costs arising from Employee's actions, with no limitation on liability amount.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
All disputes must be resolved through binding arbitration. Employee waives right to jury trial and class action lawsuits.

By signing below, Employee acknowledges reading and agreeing to all terms.
